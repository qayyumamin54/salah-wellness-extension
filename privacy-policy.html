Overview

📋

This Privacy Policy describes how the Salah & Wellness Break Chrome Extension ("the Extension", "we", "our") handles your information. The Extension was built to help Muslims combine daily prayer times with short wellness breaks — stretching, posture resets, and breathing exercises.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This extension is designed with a privacy-first approach: your data stays on your device, and we do not operate any servers or databases that store your personal information.

Simple summary: We collect the bare minimum needed to show you prayer times. Nothing leaves your device except the city name/coordinates sent to public prayer time APIs.
2

Data We Collect

📊

The Extension collects only what is strictly necessary to function:

Data Why Where Stored Shared?
City name To display your location in the popup Local only Never
Latitude & Longitude To fetch accurate prayer times from Aladhan API Local only API request only
Prayer times To schedule alarms and show countdowns Local only Never
Language preference English or Bahasa Melayu setting Local only Never
Notification style Popup or full-screen overlay preference Local only Never

We do not collect: your name, email, browsing history, visited URLs, IP address, device identifiers, or any analytics data.

3

How Your Data Is Used

⚙️

Your data is used exclusively to operate the Extension's core features:

City coordinates are sent to the Aladhan.com prayer times API and Nominatim geocoding API once per day to retrieve your local prayer schedule. These are public, open-source APIs that do not require registration and do not track users.

Prayer times are cached locally on your device for the current day so the Extension does not need to make repeated API calls.

Preferences (language, notification style) are saved locally so the Extension remembers your settings between sessions.

Your coordinates are only transmitted as part of an HTTPS API request to fetch prayer times. They are not logged, stored, or processed by us in any way beyond this single purpose.
4

Local Storage

💽

All data is stored using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API, which saves data directly to your browser on your device. This data:

— Is accessible only to this Extension, not to websites or other extensions
— Is never synced to Google's servers (we do not use chrome.storage.sync)
— Can be deleted at any time by removing the Extension from Chrome
— Is automatically cleared if you uninstall the Extension

Third-Party APIs

🌐

The Extension communicates with two external services:

Service Purpose Data Sent Privacy Policy
Aladhan.com Fetch Islamic prayer times Latitude, longitude, date aladhan.com
Nominatim
(OpenStreetMap)		 Convert city name to coordinates City name search query OSM Foundation

These services are used via standard HTTPS requests. We have no control over their individual privacy practices. Both are widely used, community-oriented services with no known user-tracking behaviour. No authentication tokens or API keys that could be linked to you are sent.

6

Chrome Permissions

🔑

The Extension requests the following Chrome permissions, each used strictly as described:

Permission Why We Need It
storage Save your city, prayer times, and preferences locally on your device.
alarms Schedule prayer time alerts that fire at the correct time each day.
notifications Show a Chrome popup notification when prayer time arrives (if Popup mode is selected).
scripting Inject the full-screen wellness break overlay onto the active tab when prayer time arrives.
activeTab Access the currently active browser tab to display the overlay on it.
tabs Query the active tab to determine where to show the overlay.
The scripting and activeTab permissions are used only at the moment a prayer alarm fires, to display the wellness break overlay. We do not read the content of web pages, capture screenshots, or monitor your browsing activity in any way.
